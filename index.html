Shipwreck Diving

The Shipwreck in Coron is one of the most attraction for visiting in the Philippines. Visitors can try scuba dive to have an experience to look around and admire the shipwreck.

Mayon Volcano

The Mayon Volcano from the province of Albay on Luzon is also visited by visitors and admired to because of its perfect cone peak. Although aside from its peak, it is also an active stratovolcano so visitors aren't allowed to get near it for cautious and safety.

Puerto Princesa City Underground River

The PPC Underground River is chosen as one of the New Seven Wonders of Nature, this is because of its spectacular limestone within the cave. New discoveries had also have been found inside the cave such as minerals, fossils and crystals formations.

Siargao

There are a lot of iconic lagoons in Siargao that is most visited by influencers, and artists for the location's true natural beauty. Lagoons and clear sea is what Siargao has to offer, people can tour, cliff jump, kayak and paddleboard here.

Vigan City

This City is also one of the most visited city in the Philippines, because of its Spanish colonial and Asian architecture. It is mesmerizing to look at the surroundings especially of how the city had been influenced and it still preserved as to this day.

El Nido

El Nido is one of the best tourists spots and most visited amongs tourists and locals in the province of Palawan. El Nido is known to its fresh, clear sea and amazing islands and lagoons.
